BLT Recipe

Description

This can be made with just water, sugar, and instant coffee. Your reward will be a nice tall glass of cold, creamy, perfectly sweetened coffee. If you don't have a hand mixer, no worries; all you need is a humble regular whisk, one strong arm, and a good dose of patience and endurance. Just be sure you're ready for the burn in your triceps and biceps. Dalgona fluff from this recipe makes enough for one or two servings, depending on how much of a caffeine rush you're after. It tastes great but is really strong, so use caution when drinking!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp. instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp. cold water
  • Ice, for serving
  • Milk, for serving

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
  2. Combine the water, coffee, and sugar in a medium-sized bowl. Beat the mixture vigorously with a hand mixer or whisk until it becomes shiny and silky, then beat it some more until it thickens and keeps its frothy, lofty shape.
  3. Pour a glass halfway full of ice and milk, then top with a dollop and swirl of the whipped coffee mixture. Stir, if preferred, before serving.
